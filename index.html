Welcome to
Kalpataru Agrocom

Since 1975, KALPATARU AGROCOM has been providing its customers the products of unmatched quality and uncompromised taste. We are located in UNJHA, GUJARAT which is the largest business hub for agro commodities in Asia. We directly buy agro products form Farmer and processed at our Manufacturing Unit with seeds Pre-cleaners, fully mechanically sesame hulling Plant & Buhler make color sortex machine in under one unit, which results into pure quality products and this quality products we deliver to our valuable buyers. We don't just sell spices, we share part of Indian kitchen culture and traditions, making them accessible and understandable to our customers around the world. We are manufacturer, exporter and supplier of Organic & conventional Indian Spices , Herbs , Raisins , Grains , oil seeds , other Agro Products etc. That are commonly required in your daily life.
