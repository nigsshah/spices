Our Vision

The vision statement reflects the company's goal of bringing the traditional flavors and aromas of Indian spices to a global audience. It also highlights the company's commitment to sustainability, by connecting nature's traditions to the modern world.

Our Mision

To deliver the natural taste and aroma of Indian spices to the globe, while always committed to quality and health and wellness.The company can invest in research and development to develop new and innovative ways to process and package spices.The company can support organizations that are working to improve the lives of farmers and their communities.


About Us


Kalpataru Agrocom is a family owned export business, and we are continuously growing by leveraging Knowledge, Expertise and Experience which we have achieved since our three generations, and this has always helped us procure and deliver the best quality products in the international market.


We also own a state of the art processing unit “Kalpataru Agrocom” at Unjha in Gujarat as well as at Guntur & Nizamabd in Andra Pradesh, where all our spices after procurement are machine cleaned or sortex cleaned. Products are then packed as per the requirement of our valued buyers, and finally loaded into container. The entire process starting through procurement of raw material to processing to packing till container loading takes place under the personal supervision of our representatives.


We at Kalpatatu Agrocom are very well aware about the locational advantage of Unjha in North Gujarat, which is considered the hub for Indian Spices, Herbs and Oil Seeds Farmers from three states: Gujarat, Rajasthan and Madhya Pradesh visit Unjha, to sell their products in auction through Unjha APMC (Agriculture Produce Market Committee) market.
